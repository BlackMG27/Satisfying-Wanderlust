Conclusion

The point of this thesis was to give young Americans some options on travel and its benefits, but it turned into how much we can and probably should value stories.

Travel is just one way of telling a story, but it’s a way that can help one reach his/her biggest potential without being encumbered by one’s daily lives. It helps people listen to stories that they might never encountered in their influence spheres or online.

Travel just moves you, and it is something that, thankfully, will only grow in popularity as more people learn what stories they can tell.
